# PROJECT DESCRIPTION
## Agentic AI × Blockchain — Challenge #1
## Autonomous DeFi Monitoring Agent

---

### Problem Statement
Decentralized Finance (DeFi) ecosystems operate continuously and experience rapid fluctuations in liquidity, token prices, and smart contract risks. Users must manually monitor multiple protocols to manage investments, detect potential threats, and respond to market opportunities. This manual monitoring is inefficient and often results in missed opportunities or financial losses. An intelligent monitoring system powered by AI and integrated with blockchain event tracking can provide real-time insights and automated alerts.

---

### Challenge
Design an autonomous AI agent capable of continuously monitoring DeFi protocols, analyzing blockchain transaction data and market conditions, and providing real-time alerts about potential risks or profitable opportunities. The system should integrate with smart contracts and blockchain networks to detect significant financial events and support proactive decision-making for users.

---

### Requirements
- AI-based monitoring of DeFi market conditions and protocol activity
- Real-time blockchain event tracking for transactions and liquidity changes
- Detection of potential risks such as liquidation threats, abnormal activity, or contract anomalies
- Automated alert system notifying users of critical financial events
- Dashboard displaying portfolio insights, risk indicators, and market trends
- Integration with blockchain wallets for user-specific monitoring
- Transparent logging of monitoring results and alerts on blockchain

---

### Impact
Improves user safety and decision-making in decentralized finance by providing intelligent monitoring, reduces financial risks caused by delayed responses, and enables proactive management of digital assets in rapidly evolving DeFi markets.
